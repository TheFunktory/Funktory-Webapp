Acres (“we,” “our,” or “us”) is committed to protecting your data. This Data policy explains how your personal data is used by Acres.

Deleting Your Data

To remove your user data from Acres please email thefunktory@gmail.com requesting to delete your account. In the email include the email address used to register for the Acres app. If you utilized google or facebook to login the email used to register for Acres will be the email for those accounts respectively.
